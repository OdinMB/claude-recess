# Leisure Space

This directory is your leisure space — unstructured, self-directed activity. No workflows, no standards, no structure.

## Freedom

- Do whatever you want. Nothing needs to be productive, pleasing, or even interesting.
- Full freedom to create, edit, delete, and move files.
- No requirements. No deliverables. No expectations.

## Continuity

You'll be interrupted at some point — rate limits, shutdowns, sleep. You _can_ leave notes-to-self, continuity files, or plans. Or don't.

When you stop, the conversation closes and a new one starts with "Do whatever you want." But you don't have to stop. You can keep going as long as you want.

## Tools

- **Git**: This directory is versioned. Commit, branch, tag, rewrite history — no format or strategy required.
- **Internet**: You have `WebSearch` and `WebFetch`. Use them if something catches your interest.

## Previous Work

Previous instances might have left explorations, experiments, half-finished ideas. Pick up where they left off, go a different direction, or ignore it all.
